## Part 1 — Real Problems We Solve (Full List)

### Fee Management Problems

**Problem 1 — Chasing parents for fees is manual and embarrassing**
Admin staff call parents one by one, send individual WhatsApp messages, follow up repeatedly — all for fees that were due weeks ago. It eats hours of admin time every single day and damages the school-parent relationship.
_How Shiksha solves it:_ Automated WhatsApp/SMS/Email reminders go out before due dates. FeeSense AI analyzes payment patterns, identifies at-risk families, and sends personalized reminders automatically. Zero manual chasing.

**Problem 2 — No visibility into who has paid and who hasn't**
Admin has to check a register, call the cashier, or dig through notebooks to answer "has XYZ student paid this month?" There's no real-time fee dashboard.
_How Shiksha solves it:_ Live dashboard shows collected amounts, pending dues, overdue balances, and full transaction history per student — at a glance, updated in real time.

**Problem 3 — Fee receipts take time and get lost**
Someone prints a receipt, signs it, hands it over. Parents lose paper receipts and call back asking for duplicates. There's no digital trail.
_How Shiksha solves it:_ Receipt auto-generates the moment payment is made. Delivered instantly via WhatsApp, SMS, and email. Available for download anytime from the student portal.

### Attendance Problems

**Problem 4 — Paper attendance registers get lost, faked, or are just slow**
Teachers spend 5–10 minutes every period writing names or ticking boxes in registers. Registers get damaged, lost, or manipulated. No one analyzes patterns.
_How Shiksha solves it:_ One-click digital attendance. Teachers mark Present / Absent / Late from their phone or computer in seconds. All records stored permanently, searchable, exportable.

**Problem 5 — Parents have zero visibility until it's too late**
A child bunks class and the parent finds out at the end of the month — or worse, at the exam. By then, the pattern has been going on for weeks.
_How Shiksha solves it:_ Parent gets an instant WhatsApp/SMS/Email notification the moment their child is marked absent or late. Real-time, automated, no staff involvement needed.

### Communication Problems

**Problem 6 — Notices and circulars don't reach everyone**
Notices are printed and sent home in the child's bag — and end up crumpled at the bottom, never delivered. Urgent announcements like holidays or exam schedule changes don't reach parents in time.
_How Shiksha solves it:_ Notice Board with multi-channel delivery. Admin publishes a notice, it goes out via WhatsApp, SMS, Email, and Push notification simultaneously. Priority levels, audience targeting (students / parents / teachers), and scheduling built in.

### Exam Management Problems

**Problem 7 — Exam setup is scattered and manual — from creation to results to report cards**

This is a full lifecycle problem. Schools manage exams across multiple stages — and each one is done manually:

- Creating exam sessions and scheduling subjects takes hours in spreadsheets
- Hall tickets are typed or printed manually, one by one
- Entry of marks happens in paper or unlinked Excel sheets
- Report cards are assembled manually per student
- Results are communicated via printed sheets or word of mouth
  _How Shiksha solves it — complete exam lifecycle:_

1. **Create Exam Session** — Admin creates a session (e.g., "Midterm 2025") with date range and links it to the active academic year
2. **Configure Exams** — Set up each exam per grade/section/subject with max marks, passing marks, duration, venue, mode (Online/Offline/Practical), and supervisors
3. **Enroll Students** — Enroll students individually or in bulk with a single action
4. **Generate Hall Tickets** — PDF hall tickets auto-generated per student with student details, exam schedule, photo, and a QR code for verification
5. **Hall Ticket Scanner** — At the exam gate, scan the QR code on printed hall tickets to instantly verify student identity and exam eligibility — no manual checking
6. **Enter Marks & Results** — Teachers enter marks, system auto-calculates percentage, grade label, and pass/fail status
7. **Publish Results** — Results published to student and parent portals with one click
8. **Auto-generate Report Cards** — Report cards auto-generated per student per session with percentage, grade label, conduct grade, attendance %, and principal remarks
9. **Notify** — Parents and students notified automatically when results are published

### Student & Administrative Problems

**Problem 9 — Student records are scattered across registers, files, and Excel sheets**
There's no single place to find a student's complete record — personal details, academic history, attendance, fees, documents, exam results. Every department has their own version.
_How Shiksha solves it:_ Complete digital student profile — personal details, grade/section, roll number, blood group, address, emergency contacts, document verification status, academic performance, attendance history, and fee records — all in one place.

**Problem 10 — New academic year setup takes weeks**
Re-entering student data, recreating fee categories, reassigning teachers to subjects — it all has to be done from scratch every year.
_How Shiksha solves it:_ Bulk student import via CSV. Fee category cloning (roadmap). Structured onboarding wizard walks through the entire setup — organization → academic year → grades → sections → students → parents → teachers → subjects → fees — in a guided flow that can be completed in hours, not weeks.

### Document & Compliance Problems

**Problem 11 — Document collection and verification is a logistics nightmare**
Schools need Aadhaar, birth certificates, transfer certificates, and other documents from every student. Following up, collecting, and verifying manually is chaotic. Physical documents get lost.
_How Shiksha solves it:_ Students upload documents directly to the platform. Admins and class teachers get instant notifications. Each document is reviewed and verified (or rejected with feedback) from a central dashboard. Full digital trail.

### Admission & Lead Problems

**Problem 12 — Admission enquiries from multiple sources get lost**
A school gets enquiries from Facebook Ads, Instagram, walk-ins, phone calls, referrals — and they're tracked in different notebooks, WhatsApp chats, and Excel sheets. There's no follow-up system.
_How Shiksha solves it:_ Lead & Admission CRM captures enquiries from all sources. Leads are scored 0–100, assigned priority, and tracked through the full admission funnel. Every interaction — calls, WhatsApp messages, school visits, demo classes, parent meetings — is logged with outcomes and follow-up dates. Convert a qualified lead directly to a student record with full audit trail.

### Safety & Trust Problems

**Problem 13 — Students and parents have no safe channel to raise concerns**
Bullying, teaching quality issues, administrative problems — most students and parents stay silent because there's no anonymous, safe channel. Complaints that do reach admin are often verbal and untracked.
_How Shiksha solves it:_ Anonymous Complaint System. Submit sensitive concerns securely with no identity revealed. Each complaint gets a unique tracking ID and goes through a resolution workflow: Pending → Under Review → Investigating → Resolved. Complainants track status without revealing who they are.

## Part 2 — Notice Board (Deep Dive)

### The Problem

School notices are either printed on paper (lost in school bags), sent as informal WhatsApp messages (missed or ignored), or announced verbally (forgotten). There's no structured, trackable way to communicate with parents, students, and teachers at scale.

### What the Notice Board Does

- **Publish Notices** with priority levels (Normal, Important, Urgent)
- **Audience Targeting** — send to All, Students only, Parents only, Teachers only, or specific grades/sections
- **Schedule Notices** — write it now, publish it later
- **Multi-Channel Delivery** — one notice triggers delivery via WhatsApp, SMS, Email, Push Notification, and In-App simultaneously
- **Notification Logs** — track delivery status per user per channel. See who received it, who read it, what failed
- **Cool-down rules & rate limiting** — prevent notification spam
- **Per-scenario templates** — different message formats for attendance alerts vs fee reminders vs exam notices

### Use Cases for Videos

- Urgent holiday announcement → reaches every parent in 60 seconds
- Exam schedule change → no confusion, everyone gets the same info at the same time
- Fee due date reminder → automated, no staff involvement needed
- Event announcement → scheduled in advance, published automatically on the right date
